About us

London Barberhood is a 4th generation barbershop where we combine traditional barbering services with modern haircuts and a chilled vibe.

We pride ourselves for being by far the best barbers on the area, combining our experience and passion to meet your highest expectations.

We just love what we do!

What we do

From the traditional cuts to the most modern blends and fades, whatever your style is, you are in good hands.
Treat yourself with the best hot towel shave in Tooting and the whole SW, definitely worth getting!
We use (and sell) Uppercut products because your hair is as important for us as it is for you.
Freshly ground coffee served daily from 7am. Have it in or take it away!
