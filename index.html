About us

London Barberhood is an award winning, 4th generation barbershop, where we distil a traditional style with modern barbering services.

We are passionate about what we do, setting high quality standards to provide you with a unique experience whilst staying true to our neighbourhood.

Our bespoke shop, professional team and chilled vibe will leave you not only looking, but also feeling great!

What we do

From your classic cuts, long hair to skin fades. We specialise in every hair type and style.
First class hot towel cut throat shaves, will leave you smooth for a week!. Beards and moustache trims plus head shaves.
We offer a wide range of Uppercut styling products because your hair is as important for us as it is for you.
Freshly ground coffee served daily from 8am. Have it in or take it away!
